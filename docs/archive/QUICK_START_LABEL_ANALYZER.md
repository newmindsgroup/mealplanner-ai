# Quick Start: Enhanced Label Analyzer 🚀

## How to Use the New Features

### 🔍 Accessing the Label Analyzer
1. Open the Meal Plan Assistant app
2. Click on **"Label Analyzer"** in the sidebar (scan icon)
3. You'll see three scan mode options

---

## 📱 Mode 1: Pantry Scan

### Purpose
Take a picture of your pantry to automatically identify and catalog all visible foods.

### Steps
1. **Select Mode**: Click the "Pantry Scan" card
2. **Take Photo**: 
   - Click "Take Photo" to use your camera
   - OR click "Upload Image" to select from your device
3. **Analyze**: Click "Analyze Pantry"
4. **Review Results**: See all identified foods with:
   - Food name
   - Category
   - Confidence percentage
   - Estimated quantity
5. **Add to Food Guide**: Click the "+ Add" button on any food item
   - Food is saved to your Food Guide
   - Button changes to "✓ Added"
   - Use these foods in meal planning!

### Best Practices
- Take photos in good lighting
- Capture multiple shelves separately for better accuracy
- Position camera straight-on (avoid angles)
- Ensure labels are visible

### What Gets Identified
- Packaged foods with visible labels
- Canned goods
- Boxed items (cereals, pasta, rice)
- Bottles and jars
- Most items with text labels

---

## 🛒 Mode 2: Grocery Store Label Scan

### Purpose
Scan product labels at the grocery store to identify harmful ingredients before purchasing.

### Steps
1. **Select Mode**: Click the "Grocery Label" card
2. **At the Store**: Take a photo of the product's ingredient list
3. **Analyze**: Click "Analyze Product"
4. **Review Results**:
   - **Health Score** (0-100): Higher is better
   - **Safety Flags**: Quick overview of concerns
   - **Harmful Ingredients**: Detailed list with:
     - Ingredient name
     - Category (additive, preservative, etc.)
     - Severity level (low/moderate/high)
     - Explanation of risks
     - Blood type conflicts (if any)
   - **Recommendations**: AI-generated advice

### Understanding the Health Score
- **80-100** (Green): Clean ingredients, healthy choice
- **60-79** (Yellow): Some concerns, consume in moderation
- **0-59** (Red): Multiple harmful ingredients, consider alternatives

### Severity Levels
- **🔴 High**: Significant health concerns (trans fats, nitrites, HFCS)
- **🟡 Moderate**: Potential issues (certain additives, preservatives)
- **⚪ Low**: Minor concerns (some artificial flavors)

### What Gets Detected
- **Additives**: MSG, BHT, BHA, TBHQ
- **Preservatives**: Sodium benzoate, nitrites, sulfites
- **Artificial Colors**: Red 40, Yellow 5, Blue 1, etc.
- **Sugars**: High fructose corn syrup, artificial sweeteners
- **Trans Fats**: Partially hydrogenated oils
- **Blood Type Conflicts**: Based on blood type diet principles

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best Scanning Results
- **Lighting**: Natural light or good indoor lighting
- **Focus**: Hold camera steady, ensure text is sharp
- **Angle**: Straight-on shots work best
- **Distance**: Close enough to read text, not too close
- **Multiple Scans**: For large pantries, scan sections

### Maximizing the Features
1. **Regular Pantry Scans**: Weekly inventory helps with meal planning
2. **Compare Products**: Scan multiple brands to find healthiest
3. **Learn About Ingredients**: Read the explanations to understand risks
4. **Blood Type Awareness**: Pay attention to compatibility warnings
5. **Save Scans**: Previous scans are saved for reference

### Integration with Food Guide
- Pantry items added to Food Guide appear in meal suggestions
- Blood type compatibility is already set
- Edit items later in Food Guide if needed
- Hide items you don't want in meal plans

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Does pantry scan work with all foods?**
A: It works best with packaged foods that have visible labels. Fresh produce without labels may not be identified.

**Q: How accurate is the food identification?**
A: Confidence scores show accuracy (usually 80-95%). Higher scores mean more certain identification.

**Q: Can I scan products in any language?**
A: Currently optimized for English labels. International product support may vary.

**Q: What if a harmful ingredient isn't detected?**
A: The system checks 30+ common harmful ingredients. Very rare or new additives may not be in the database.

**Q: How is the health score calculated?**
A: Based on number and severity of harmful ingredients. High-severity ingredients reduce score more.

**Q: Can I scan multiple items at once in pantry mode?**
A: Yes! The AI will identify all visible food items in a single photo.

**Q: Do I need internet for scanning?**
A: Yes, for AI analysis. OCR text extraction also requires processing.

**Q: Are my scans private?**
A: All scans are stored locally in your browser. Images are only sent to AI service during analysis.
