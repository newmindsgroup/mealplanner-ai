🧹 Clear LocalStorage

This will clear all saved data for the Meal Plan Assistant app, including:

  • Family member profiles
  • Meal plans
  • Grocery lists
  • Favorites
  • Settings
  • Chat history
⚠️ Warning: This action cannot be undone. You'll need to set up your profiles again after clearing.

Manual Method (Browser Console)

You can also clear localStorage manually:

  1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12 or Right-click → Inspect)
  2. Go to the Console tab
  3. Type: localStorage.clear() and press Enter
  4. Type: location.reload() to refresh the page
